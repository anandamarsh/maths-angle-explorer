Angle Explorer

Angles, Aiming & Missing Angle Reasoning

Stage 2-3 NSW Curriculum

MA2-16MG / MA3-16MG

Students rotate a cannon to match 30°, 45°, 60°, 90°, 135° and more, in both positive and negative angles.

They then solve missing-angle problems by reasoning from 90°, 180°, and 360° totals before firing.
